"Sharp onset", A sound caused by a mallet or plectrum the onset sound is closer to the character of the main body of the sound;
"Marked onset", Normal (non-legato) onset of a wind or string instrument;
"Flat onset", No particular marking of the onset as in legato playing;
"Swelled onset", A short crescendo/decrescendo;
"Fade in", Dal niente/gradual crescendo;
