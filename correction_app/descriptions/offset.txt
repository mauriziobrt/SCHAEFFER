"Sharp ending", Sounds with an accentuated ending phase; 
"Sudden stop", Sounds stopped suddenly_ rather than rounded off; 
"Flat ending", No particular characteristic regarding the ending; 
"Soft ending", The ending of the sustained sound is rounded off with a diminuendo; 
"Laissez vibrer",A resonating sound is let free to resonate until it dies out; 
"Interrupted resonance", A resonating sound is suddenly stopped; 
"Fade out", A fade out effect is applied to the ending of the sound object;
