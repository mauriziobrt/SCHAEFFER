"Fulfilled Forward push", Tendency directed towards a future point in time and/or by an increasing tendency.;
"Evaded forward push", In evaded forward push the goal is not reached;
"Suspended forward push",The expected goal of a forward-oriented function is suspended.; 
"Backward push", Tendency directed away from a past point in time and/or by decreasing tendency.; 
"Neutral", Vague tendency or conflicting tendencies;
"Glissando Up", Upward glissando.;
"Glissando Down", Downward glissando;
"Glissando Complex", Mixed direction glissando;
"Evanescent appereance", Sound that appear and disappears out of nowhere.;
